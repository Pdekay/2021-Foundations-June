About

Locally owned and operated, we have been serving the greater Omaha area since 1987. We started out grooming our family, and friends dogs in our basement shop, to three facilties throughout the metro area.

We have numerous awards and have placed nationally in several competitions over the years. We cater to breeds all shapes and sizes. We offer afforable pricing and several tiers of memeberships depending or your pets grooming needs.

While you wait please feel free to browse our shop full of hand crafted pet toys, home made treats and much more, all from LOCAL VENDORS!!! We grew up locally owned and supported and its part of who we are, we always try to support local when possible. If you would like to help donate, please ask the cashier to round your ticket to the nearest dollar to help support local shelters.
